# Project Vision

## Vision

CareerPilot AI aims to become an intelligent career operating system that helps students throughout their placement journey using multiple AI agents instead of a traditional chatbot.

## Target Users

- College Students
- Fresh Graduates
- Job Seekers
- Career Switchers

## Problem

Students use multiple websites for resumes, interview preparation, coding practice, and job searching. These disconnected platforms provide inconsistent guidance.

## Solution

CareerPilot AI integrates multiple AI agents into one platform to provide personalized, intelligent, and context-aware career assistance.

## Why Agentic AI?

Unlike a chatbot, multiple AI agents can collaborate, specialize in different tasks, and maintain context, resulting in more accurate and useful recommendations.

## Expected Outcome

Students receive personalized career guidance, improve their placement readiness, and manage their entire preparation journey through a single platform.
